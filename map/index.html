About the Eco Map

WSP Eco Map is a mobile map of the trees in Washington Square Park. The map includes a layer showing the historic aboveground flow and contemporary sewerized route of Minetta Brook. The map was funded through donations on the ioby.org platform.

The data

The mobile map was designed by GIS and web developer Casey Brown. The tree data is derived from historic and contemporary tree surveys of Washington Square Park commissioned by the NYC Parks and Recreation Department. Paper maps were copied from the map archives at the Olmsted Center in Flushing. The interactive Minetta Brook layer is from the work of Steve Duncan at undercity.org.

How to use the Eco Map

Explore (show or hide) layers

  • Click on the "Options" icon in the upper right
  • Click on "Explore"
  • Toggle which layers you would like to see

Learn about a tree

  • Click on a tree and the "About this tree" panel will open
  • If you click on a group of trees, the map will zoom in and you can then click on a single tree

Leave a note about a tree

  • Login (create an account)
  • Click on a tree and the "About this tree" panel will open
  • Add a comment
