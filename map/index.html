    About WSP Eco Map

    WSP Eco Projects (formerly WSP Ecology)

    WSP Eco Map is a mobile map of the trees in the park, the historic flow and contemporary route of the channelized Minetta Brook/Creek, and other environmental assets in the park such as nesting boxes.

    The data

    The mobile map was designed by GIS and web developer Casey Brown. The tree data is derived from historic and contemporary tree surveys of Washington Square Park commissioned by the NYC Parks and Recreation Department. Paper maps were copied from the map archives at the Olmsted Center in Flushing. The interactive Minetta Brook is from the work of Steve Duncan at undercity.org.

    How to use the mobile map

    Explore (show or hide) layers

    • Click on the "Options" icon in the upper right
    • Click on "Explore"
    • Toggle which layers you would like to see

    Learn about a tree

    • Click on a tree and the "About this tree" panel will open
    • If you click on a group of trees, the map will zoom in and you can then click on a single tree

    Leave a note about a tree

    • Login (create an account)
    • Click on a tree and the "About this tree" panel will open
    • Add a comment
